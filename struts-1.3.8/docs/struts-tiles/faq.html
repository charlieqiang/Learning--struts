FAQ

  1. Can I use Tiles without Struts?
  2. Why are there two versions?
Can I use Tiles without Struts?

Yes. Stand-alone Tiles is currently in the Struts Sandbox, and nightly builds are available here.

Why are there two versions?

Stand-alone Tiles is still under development. Until it emerges from the Sandbox, the Struts Action Framework will continue to depend on Struts Tiles.
